The Problem

Creators build protocols.
Protocols keep the money.

A single thread by a Solana-native creator can drive hundreds of wallet activations, millions in TVL, and protocol breakout moments. Yet the creator receives zero attribution for that downstream economic activity. The connection between content influence and protocol value is invisible — and that invisibility is structural, not accidental.

Today's creator monetization relies on sponsorships negotiated after the fact, platform ad revenue capped at pennies, and tip jars that require active friction from readers. None of these models capture the compounding, permissionless, verifiable value that great crypto content actually generates on-chain.

With no standard for content attribution on Solana, protocols can't reward the creators who drive their growth even if they wanted to. The result: the creators who matter most to ecosystem health are the least compensated for it.

The Solution

Attribution infrastructure
built into the chain itself.

Inkdrop deploys an Anchor program that stores a SHA-256 fingerprint of your content in a Metaplex Bubblegum compressed NFT — costing fractions of a cent. A configurable royalty DAG lives in an Anchor PDA alongside the fingerprint, defining how value flows to you and any co-creators at basis-point precision.

When a downstream protocol includes your Inkdrop content fingerprint in a transaction memo field — using our open attribution memo standard — a Helius-powered indexer detects it in real time and triggers a CPI call that routes 5–15% of associated value into your claimable treasury PDA.

No oracles. No trusted intermediaries. The entire attribution proof chain is on-chain and auditable by anyone, at any time. Protocols adopt the standard by adding fewer than 10 lines of TypeScript to their existing instruction builders.

How It Works

From content to royalty
in four on-chain steps.

1

Anchor Your Content

Paste a URL or markdown into the Inkdrop app. Connect your wallet, sign once, and a compressed NFT is minted with your content's SHA-256 fingerprint and royalty DAG.

2

Protocols Cite You

Any Solana protocol using the Inkdrop SDK embeds your content fingerprint in their transaction memo field — a one-time integration that permanently credits your influence.

3

Attribution Fires

Helius detects the attribution event via webhook. The Anchor program verifies the fingerprint match and executes a CPI call, routing royalties to your treasury PDA trustlessly.

4

Claim Your Earnings

Open your dashboard, see every citing transaction and protocol, and sweep all pending royalties into your wallet in a single batch transaction. Your influence, monetized.
